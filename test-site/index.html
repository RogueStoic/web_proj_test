Vikings are awesome

10 reasons why vikings are awesome

You may have seen the popular TV Series Vikings, which gives some dramatic insights into the epic struggles and adventures of the early Viking age. We took a deep dive into some more historical findings on these "barbaric" raiders and traders, and have compiled a list of 10 things that created the vibrant and brutal viking culture we can still learn from today. So put on your best chainmail, tap a fresh horn of mead, and sit down for this short primer that will see you cross oceans and pillage churches in no time!

1: Every Viking was a one-man war machine

When it came to war, few were equally trained as fierce warriors as those born in the bays and fjords of Norse Scandinavia. From a young age, a viking would learn how to:

  • Use an axe and shield in mock-fighting
  • Hunt and survive far away from home
  • Weather some of the harshest climatic conditions in Europe
